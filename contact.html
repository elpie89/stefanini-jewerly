Our Locations

Stefanini Jewellery follows the rhythm of the seasons, choosing destinations that reflect our vision of elegance and beauty.

Summer — Porto Cervo

Since 2023, we have been present in Porto Cervo, at the prestigious Promenade du Port in the heart of Costa Smeralda.

A vibrant and sophisticated destination that represents our ideal summer setting.

Winter — Gstaad

In winter, we move to Gstaad, within the Palace Hotel.

A timeless, intimate and refined environment that perfectly embodies the spirit of Stefanini Jewellery.

Two iconic locations, one vision.
